Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a study group?

You can create a study group by going to the Groups section and clicking on "Create New Group". Fill in the details and invite your friends to join!

Is Studymate free to use?

Yes! Studymate is completely free to use. We believe education should be accessible to everyone.

How can I share resources?

You can share resources by uploading files in the Resources section. Supported formats include PDF, DOC, PPT, and images.

Can I use Studymate on mobile?

Absolutely! Studymate is fully responsive and works great on all devices including smartphones and tablets.
